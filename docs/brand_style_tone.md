## 1. Brand Personality
- Technical but friendly  
- Reliable  
- Forward-thinking  
- Practical  
- Clean and modern  

Tone:  
- Direct  
- Confident  
- Minimal  

Avoid:  
- Buzzwords  
- Overly emotional language  

---

## 2. Voice Guidelines

### Clear
Explain features plainly:
> Workflow Ops shows you what changed before deployment.

### Confident
> Built for teams who automate at scale.

### No-fluff
Do not add hype or filler language.

---

## 3. Visual Identity

### Logo
A geometric A-shape inside a circle.  
Modern, minimal, unmistakable.

Color palette:
- Primary Blue: `#4A6BAC`  
- Accent White  
- Dark Neutral for typography  

Typography:
- Sans-serif (Inter, Roboto, or similar)  
- Tight spacing  
- Minimal use of italics or decorative styles  

---

## 4. Layout Guidelines
- Generous spacing  
- Minimal borders  
- Clear sections  
- Limited color usage  

---

## 5. Imagery
- Abstract automation diagrams  
- Clean lines  
- Subtle gradients  

No stock photos of people.

---

## 6. Messaging Pillars
- Reliability  
- Clarity  
- Governing automation  
- Visibility  
- Speed of execution
